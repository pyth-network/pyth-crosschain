Entropy v2 contracts are now live on Arbitrum and Base, bringing lower request
fees and configurable callback gas limits to both networks.

Highlights:

- `requestV2` with per-request gas overrides
- Provider-configurable fee schedules
- Batch reveal for high-volume apps

See [contract addresses](/entropy/contract-addresses) for the new deployments.
