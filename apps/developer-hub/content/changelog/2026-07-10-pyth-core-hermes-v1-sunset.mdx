title: Hermes v1 price endpoint sunset date set for October 1
date: 2026-07-10
product: pyth-core
type: breaking-change
area: apis
---

The legacy Hermes `/v1/updates/price` endpoints will be **removed on October 1,
2026**. After that date, requests to v1 routes return `410 Gone`.

What you should do:

- Migrate to `/v2/updates/price/latest` and `/v2/updates/price/stream`.
- If you rely on `verbose=true` metadata, switch to the v2 `parsed` field.
- Benchmarks endpoints are unaffected.

The [migration guide](/price-feeds/core/migrate-an-app-to-pyth) covers the
response-shape differences in detail.
